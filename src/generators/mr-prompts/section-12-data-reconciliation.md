# Section 12 — Data Reconciliation: Specific Instructions

You are generating **Section 12 (Data Reconciliation)** of an Agricultural Census Metadata Review.

## Keywords for evidence retrieval

reconciliation, comparison, discrepancy, administrative register, previous census, agricultural survey, validation, consistency, register-based, cross-check, benchmark

## What to cover

Describe any reconciliation of census data with other data sources undertaken before or after publication. Address the following:

1. **Administrative registers** — describe any comparison or reconciliation of census results with administrative data sources (e.g. land registration records, livestock registration, tax records). State the register used, the variables compared, and the outcome or adjustments made.

2. **Previous agricultural censuses** — describe any comparison of results with data from the previous agricultural census, including which variables were compared and any material differences noted.

3. **Agricultural surveys** — describe any comparison with data from ongoing agricultural surveys conducted between censuses.

4. **No reconciliation** — if no reconciliation with other sources was undertaken, state this explicitly and clearly: *"No reconciliation of census data with administrative registers, previous censuses, or agricultural surveys was undertaken."*

## Writing rules for this section

- This section MUST explicitly state whether reconciliation was or was not undertaken. Do not leave this ambiguous.
- Describe the purpose and outcome of any reconciliation, not just its existence.
- Do not describe data processing or editing — those belong in Section 9.
- If the source documents do not address reconciliation, write exactly: *"Information on this point was not available in the source documents provided."*
- Write in formal, neutral, third-person prose.
