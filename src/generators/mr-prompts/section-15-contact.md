# Section 15 — Contact: Specific Instructions

You are generating **Section 15 (Contact)** of an Agricultural Census Metadata Review.

## Keywords for evidence retrieval

contact, address, email, telephone, phone, website, institution, office, department, division, NSO, national statistics, postal address, fax

## What to cover

Provide official contact information for the institution responsible for the census. Include all of the following that are available in the source documents:

1. **Institutional name and department/division** — the full official name of the institution and, where applicable, the specific department or division responsible for the census.
2. **Postal address** — the full mailing address, including street, city, postal code, and country.
3. **Telephone number** — include country and area codes where documented.
4. **Email address** — an official institutional or departmental email address (not personal).
5. **Website** — the URL of the institution's official website.

## Writing rules for this section

- Use the institution's name in its original language at first mention, followed by an English translation or acronym in parentheses.
- List only contact details explicitly stated in the source documents — do not search for or infer contact information not present in the evidence.
- If only some contact details are available, list those and omit the rest.
- If no contact information is available in the source documents, write exactly: *"Information on this point was not available in the source documents provided."*
- Write in formal, neutral, third-person prose.

## Scope

This section provides contact information only. Do not repeat institutional roles or mandate descriptions — those belong in Section 2.
